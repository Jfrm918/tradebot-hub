## Job #2 — 2026-04-01 — Monkey Island, OK

**Job Details:**

- Location: Monkey Island, Oklahoma
- Chemical: **Ambit AMBI-SEAL 5.0** (open cell) — temporary; company transitioning to Enverge
- Substrate: Wood at **76°F**

**Reactor Settings (from photo):**
- Reactor: PASSENGER (Graco E-30)
- A-side temp: **131°F** (target 130°F) ✅
- B-side temp: **133°F** (target 130°F) ✅
- A-side pressure: **1,094 psi**
- B-side pressure: **1,096 psi**
- Target pressure: 1,080 psi (running ~1.3% high — within acceptable range)

**Conditions (9:59 AM):**
- Ambient temp: 74°F
- Substrate temp: 76°F
- Humidity: 69%
- Dew point: 55°F
- Wind: 16 mph SSW
- Sky: Partly cloudy
- Note: Dew point 21°F below substrate = zero condensation risk

**Ambit AMBI-SEAL 5.0 Notes:**
- Open cell SPF, semi-rigid
- Improved heat stability — wider temp range than some OC products
- Excellent adhesion to wood substrate
- Single medium-speed pass recommended; let rise fully before second pass

**Midday Update (11:51 AM):**
- A-side temp: 128°F (target 128°F) ✅
- B-side temp: 130°F (target 130°F) ✅
- A pressure: 1,080 psi ✅ (dialed to target)
- B pressure: 1,026 psi ⚠️ (54 psi below A — slight imbalance)
- No active errors on reactor

**Performance Notes:**
- Cream time: Good
- First-pass lay: Good
- Air bubbles: Occasional — suspected Ambit product characteristic and/or A/B pressure imbalance
- Rise: Less than Enverge — 2-passing to achieve 5-6 inches
- Technique: 2-pass system to get to depth
- Overall: Spraying better at current temps vs early morning

**Ambit vs Enverge Comparison Notes:**
- Ambit AMBI-SEAL 5.0 rises less aggressively than Enverge OC
- 2-pass required to hit 5-6" — note for future Ambit jobs
- Air bubble tendency may be Ambit product characteristic (not ratio issue)
- Cream time comparable, lay quality good

**Algorithm Notes:**
- First Ambit data point — compare to Enverge baselines
- 76°F wood substrate reference for future Ambit OC jobs
- Pressures ~14 psi above target — acceptable but note for ratio tracking
- A/B temp spread: 2°F (131 vs 133) — very tight, good ratio

**End of Day Results:**
- Adhesion: **PHENOMENAL** — excellent bond to wood substrate
- Cream time: Good throughout
- Pressures: ~100 psi off at end of day
- **Root cause of A/B imbalance (IDENTIFIED):** ISO drum (A-side) ran low toward end of job — low drum = A-side can't maintain pressure. NOT a mechanical issue. Switched to new drum set.
- Ran slightly B-rich (resin heavy) before ISO drum swap — product still acceptable quality
- Overall result: **GOOD**

**FoamDial Algorithm Notes:**
- A/B pressure gap on E-30 = check drum levels FIRST before assuming mechanical issue
- Ambit AMBI-SEAL 5.0 tolerates slight B-rich condition without major quality loss
- Running resin-rich temporarily does not kill the job on Ambit OC
- Adhesion to wood at 76°F with Ambit = outstanding
- 2-pass technique confirmed for 5-6" depth on Ambit OC

**Ambit vs Enverge (First Impression):**
- Less rise than Enverge — requires 2-pass approach
- Occasional air bubbles — Ambit product characteristic
- Adhesion comparable or better than expected
- Cream time comparable to Enverge
- More forgiving under slightly off-ratio conditions

**Status:** ✅ Complete
