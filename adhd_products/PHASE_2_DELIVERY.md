## Phase 2A: Competitive Research Findings

### KDP Bestseller Patterns
- **Top Titles:** The ADHD Planner (undated), ADHD Daily Schedule Organizer, Astral Planner, CanPlan Mental Health
- **Price Range:** $7–15 (paperback), $4–7 (Kindle)
- **Design:** Minimalist, clean fonts, black/white/neutral base with warm accents
- **Key Keywords:** undated, simple, brain-friendly, no perfectionism, structured-yet-flexible
- **What Converts:** Eliminating perfectionist guilt, affirmations, symptom tracking, self-care sections

### Etsy Bestseller Patterns
- **Top Templates:** Ultimate ADHD Organizer Binder, Future ADHD Digital Planner, ADHD Infinite Focus
- **Price Range:** $8–25 (templates), $15–30 (bundles)
- **Design:** Customizable colors (ocean blue, sunset orange, sage green), minimalist grids, warm palettes
- **What Works:** Bundles (3-5 items), printable PDFs, digital versions (GoodNotes/Notability), "Made by an ADHDer" positioning
- **Aesthetic:** Professional coach vibe, not overly designed, calm palettes, warm colors ONLY (no harsh grays)

### Universal Success Factors
✅ **Undated** (removes failure if you skip days)
✅ **Weekly + Daily views** (structure without overwhelm)
✅ **Brain dumps** (space for scattered thoughts)
✅ **Habit tracking** (simple, not complex)
✅ **Minimalist design** (ADHD brains are visually sensitive)
✅ **Warm colors** (soft blues, creams, warm oranges—NOT harsh)
✅ **Simple typography** (no cursive, clean sans-serif)
✅ **Affirmations & self-care sections** (emotional regulation)
✅ **Made by someone who gets it** (authentic positioning)
✅ **Emotion tracking + mood support** (comorbidity reality)
