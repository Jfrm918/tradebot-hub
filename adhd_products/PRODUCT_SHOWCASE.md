## Platform Strategy

### KDP: Passive Income + Volume
- 40 books at $6.99–$12.99
- Once printed, passive revenue
- Low production cost
- Seasonal demand (back-to-school, New Year)
- SEO/keyword advantage (Amazon search)

### Etsy: Impulse Buyers + Customization
- 20 printable listings
- Instant digital download
- Impulse-friendly price points ($7.99–$12.99)
- Bundles (3–5 items) increase AOV
- Customization options (colors) = buyer loyalty

### Gumroad: Premium Buyers + Creator Community
- 8 high-value digital products ($17.99–$27.99)
- Notion templates, AI prompts, video courses
- Creators/solopreneurs as audience
- Premium positioning = quality perceived
- Coaching/masterclass = high perceived value

## Design Consistency Across All 68

### Color System
- **Warm Cream** — Primary background (safe, calm, paper-like)
- **Soft Blue** — Professional, focus, calmness
- **Warm Orange** — Energy, approachability, warmth
- **Sage Green** — Nature, grounding, stability
- **Soft Lavender** — Self-care, therapeutic, healing

### Typography
- **No cursive** (ADHD accessibility)
- Clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Inter, Roboto)
- Generous spacing (visual breathing room)
- Hierarchy clear (headers < body < notes)

### Layout Principles
- Minimalist (no visual clutter)
- Plenty of white space
- Simple grids & structures
- Affirmations throughout
- Self-compassion baked in
